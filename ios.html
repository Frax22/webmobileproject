iOS

Apple's Mobile Operating System

The second most used Mobile Operating System in the world

iPadOS is the version for tablets
watchOS is for smartwatches
tvOS is for Apple TVs

History

  • 2007iPhone OS 1

    The first iPhone OS version included multitouch screen, an integrated voicemail, iTunes.

  • 2008iPhone OS 2

    iPhone OS 2.0 was released with App Store, push notification, compatibility with Microsoft Exchange servers, new features in Mail and Contacts.

  • 2009Iphone OS 3

    The third and last version of iPhoneOS coincided with the launch of iPhone 3GS, which introduces: Spotlight global search, MMS support, video recording with Camera app, copy paste, the ability to record voice messages, support for MobileMe (pre iCloud online services).

  • 2010iOS 4

    iOS 4 arrives with the new nomenclature and free for all devices and all users. The date corresponds with the release of the iPads, which will use the same software (until 2019).
    Some of the added features were: FaceTime, AirPlay, hotspot, multitasking, spell checking, the unified inbox, digital zoom in the Camera app, creating folders, changeable Home screen wallpapers.

  • 2011iOS 5

    iOS 5 was released including: iCloud, iMessage, Notification, Siri.

  • 2012iOS 6

    With the launch of iOS 6: the Google Maps app was removed it was replaced by Apple Maps, the Facebook app was integrated, Improvements were made to Siri and FaceTime.
    iOS 6 is remembered for being one of the company's failures beacuse it had a lot of issues with basic functionality and there were a lot of inconsistencies in the choices for user interface and interactions.

  • 2013iOS 7

    iOS 7 was launched, which radically changed the graphic appearance.
    “Skeuomorphism” (something is designed with extra ornamentation to make it resemble another object so it is more familiar) was abandoned to make everything simpler and more minimal (flat design).
    Also introduced: AirDrop, to transfer files, Control Center, support for Touch ID, a new tab-based Multitasking interface.

  • 2014iOS 8

    iOS 8 was launched, including: Apple Music, Handoff (to continue the activity on other devices, HomeKit, Apple Pay, Editor integrated into the Photos app, the family sharing function, voice messages on iMessage, Health app.

  • 2015iOS 9

    iOS 9 included: new Apple News app, new multitasking functions on iPad (with Slide Over, Split View and Picture in Picture), Night Shift (a function for automatically adjusting the color temperature of screens), energy saving.
    iOS 9 was received positively because it wanted to make the operating system more solid, in terms of performance and robustness.

  • 2016iOS 10

    Introduces some new features: redesigned Maps app, the Memories function in the Photos app, Siri support for third-party apps, Home application for controlling smart appliances.

  • 2017iOS 11

    iOS 11 focuses on iPads, to make the products more attractive as replacements for laptops.
    Among the new features, especially for developers: new features for Siri, such as translations and a more natural voice thanks to machine learning, CoreML for machine learning was also introduced, ARKit for augmented reality.

  • 2018iOS 12

    Adds support for ARKit 2, improvements for Siri, introduction of shortcuts, Memojis, device usage time via Screen Time.

  • 2019iOS 13

    iOS 13 introduced numerous new features: the system no longer runs on iPad, Dark Mode, improved user privacy, recognition with FaceID.

  • 2020iOS 14

    iOS 14 introduced: Widgets are arriving, picture in picture, Privacy and Data improvements, redesigned Messages and Maps apps.

  • 2021iOS 15

    iOS 15 introduced: Face ID (even with a mask), SharePlay (in FaceTime calls you will be able to share your screen), New Memojis, “Full immersion” mode to help you concentrate, notification summary

  • 2022iOS 16

    Different improvements: AirDrop, lock screen, improved focus mode, improved Visual Search, spoken content.

  • 2023iOS 17

    Several improvements: interactive widgets, wallpaper improvements, StandBy mode, autocorrect and text prediction improvements, AirDrop improvements, Faster Haptic Touch
